Who We Are

Crafting Homes,
Building Legacies

BuildNest was born in 2010 from a simple yet powerful belief — every family deserves a home built with integrity, quality, and care. Our founder, Vikram Khanna, started with a small team of 5 passionate engineers and a commitment to doing things right.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown into a full-spectrum construction and real estate company, but our core values remain unchanged. We still treat every project as if it were our own home. Every brick we lay, every beam we raise, carries the weight of our reputation and the trust our clients place in us.

Our Mission: To deliver world-class construction with complete transparency, making the dream of homeownership accessible and joyful for every family.

Our Vision: To be India's most trusted home construction partner, pioneering sustainable building practices and smart home technologies that define the future of living.
